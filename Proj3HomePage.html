Trainer Of The Month:
Gary Oak


Gary Oak is Ash's rival. He first appeared in Pokemon - I Choose You!, where he left Pallet Town and wanted to become a Pokemon Master before Ash did. In the beginning, Gary claimed to be the best of the best and had the typical smug attitude, known by having fangirls and a fanclub to go along, but, as the anime moved forward, he became less prideful and arrogant than that of his earlier appearances in the anime.

In later seasons, while Gary still retains his somewhat arrogant attitude, he's a much kinder and friendlier person and is considerably more polite and respectful to other trainers.
